Sri Radhai Industries - Company Profile

Established in 2000, Sri Radhai Industries is a trusted manufacturer of precision-engineered mechanical, mechatronic and electrical components serving agricultural equipment and truck OEMs.

Core Strengths:

  • 25+ skilled employees with strong production experience
  • Quality, reliability and innovation-driven execution
  • Timely supply for complex manufacturing requirements
  • Modern machinery and testing-oriented workflow

Product Range:

  • Agricultural equipment parts
  • Truck components
  • Mechatronic assemblies
  • Electrical assemblies

Major Customer Segments:

  • Zetra
  • VST Tillers Tractors Ltd
  • Monarch Tractor
  • Ashok Leyland
  • Simpson & Co Ltd

Vision & Commitment:

  • Expand capabilities in mechanical, mechatronic and electrical manufacturing
  • Invest in state-of-the-art machinery and workforce development
  • Maintain competitiveness against global OEM standards
  • Operate with integrity, innovation and technical excellence

Sri Radhai Industries stands as a reliable OEM partner for comprehensive component solutions across mechanical, mechatronic and electrical domains.
